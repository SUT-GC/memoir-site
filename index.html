Welcome to Memoir (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). We take your privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

1. Information Collection

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Journal Content: Text entries and mood records you create
  • Voice Recordings: Audio journals and speech-to-text transcriptions
  • Photos: Images you add to your journal entries
  • Location Data: Geographic tags you choose to add (only when authorized)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Used for iCloud sync identification
  • Usage Statistics: In-app statistics such as entry count and streak days
Important: This app does not collect your name, phone number, email address, or other personally identifiable information.

2. Use of Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • Storing and displaying your journal content on your local device
  • Syncing data across your devices via iCloud
  • Speech-to-text functionality (using Apple's Speech framework, supports offline processing)
  • AI journal summary feature (using third-party AI service to generate summaries)
  • Spotlight search indexing for quick journal lookup

3. Data Storage & Security

3.1 Local Storage

Your journal data is primarily stored locally on your device, managed using Apple's SwiftData framework.

3.2 iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud, your data will be encrypted and synced through Apple's iCloud service. iCloud data security is guaranteed by Apple.

3.3 App Lock

This app supports Face ID / Touch ID / device passcode protection, providing an additional layer of privacy.

3.4 AI Services

When you use the AI summary feature, your journal content is sent to a third-party AI service (Deepseek) for processing. We only send the necessary text content for generating summaries; your photos and personal identity information are never transmitted.

4. Information Sharing

We commit to:

  • Never selling your personal data to any third party
  • Never sharing your data with advertisers
  • Never using your data for marketing purposes

We do not share your information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • With your explicit consent
  • When required by law
  • When using the AI summary feature, necessary text content is sent to the AI service provider

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: You can view all your journal entries at any time
  • Modify: You can edit any journal entry
  • Delete: You can delete individual or all entries
  • Export: You can export all data through the Settings page
  • Revoke Permissions: You can revoke microphone, photos, location, and other permissions in system settings at any time

6. Permissions

This app may request the following permissions:

  • Microphone: For recording voice journals
  • Speech Recognition: For converting speech to text
  • Photo Library: For adding photos to journals
  • Face ID / Touch ID: For app lock protection
  • Location Services: For recording journal locations (optional)

All permissions are optional. You can choose which ones to grant. Declining permissions will not affect basic app functionality.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you discover that we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us, and we will immediately delete the relevant data.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated policies will be posted on this page with the revision date noted. For significant changes, we will notify you within the app.
