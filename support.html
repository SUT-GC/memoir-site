FAQ

Q: Where is my journal data stored?

A: Your journal data is primarily stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud, data will be encrypted and synced through Apple's iCloud service across all your devices.

Q: How can I recover deleted entries?

A: Currently, deleted entries cannot be directly recovered. We recommend regularly backing up your journals using the "Export Data" feature in Settings. If you have iCloud enabled, you may try restoring from an iCloud backup.

Q: Does speech-to-text require internet?

A: Speech-to-text uses Apple's Speech framework and supports offline recognition. You may need to download a language pack when using a language for the first time.

Q: How does AI Summary work?

A: After saving an entry, the system automatically sends your text content to an AI service to generate a brief summary. Only text content is processed; your photos are never uploaded.

Q: How do I enable App Lock?

A: Go to Settings, find "App Lock" and enable it. The app supports Face ID, Touch ID, or device passcode authentication.

Q: Can I use the app without an iCloud account?

A: Yes. Without an iCloud account, data is stored locally and all features work normally. You just won't have multi-device sync.
